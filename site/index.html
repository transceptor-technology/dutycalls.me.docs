    Welcome to DutyCalls

    DutyCalls is a notification routing tool made to make notifications more visible to its audience. Many organizations struggle with the fact that notifications are not viewed on time or not viewed at all by their target audience. This can lead to unforeseen circumstances. DutyCalls is here to solve that problem. DutyCalls has a mobile and web application to stay up to date on the latest notifications in a very user-friendly and intuitive way. In addition, integrating your system with DutyCalls has never been easier. For more information about our product, please go to dutycalls.me.
